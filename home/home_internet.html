About the INTERNET?

The Internet is a global network that connects millions of computers and devices around the world. It allows people to share information, communicate instantly, and access countless resources anytime, anywhere.



History of the INTERNET?

The Internet began in the late 1960s as a project called ARPANET, created by the United States government to connect a few computers for research. In the 1980s, it started to grow and connect universities and organizations around the world.



News & Updates

The Internet is always changing with new technologies and trends appearing every day. Today, people can get the latest news and updates instantly from websites, social media, and online news channels. From global events to new apps and inventions, the Internet helps us stay informed and connected.
